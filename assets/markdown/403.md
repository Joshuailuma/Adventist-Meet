# 403 - Let Us Break Bread Together
-------------------
## Key Eb Major


**1**  
Let us break bread together on our knees;  
Let us break bread together on our knees.    

**2**   
Let us drink wine together on our knees;  
Let us drink wine together on our knees.   

**3**  
Let us praise God together on our knees;  
Let us praise God together on our knees.
