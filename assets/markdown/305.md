# 305 - Give Me Jesus
-------------------
## Key C Major


**1**  
In the morning when I rise,  
In the morning when I rise,  
In the morning when I rise,  
Give me Jesus. 


### Refrain  
Give me Jesus, Give me Jesus,  
You may have all this world,  
Give me Jesus.
   
**2**  
Dark midnight was my cry,  
Dark midnight was my cry,  
Dark midnight was my cry,  
Give me Jesus.   

**3**  
Just about the break of day,  
Just about the break of day,  
Just about the break of day,  
Give me Jesus. 

**4**  
Oh, when I come to die,  
Oh, when I come to die,  
Oh, when I come to die,  
Give me Jesus.
