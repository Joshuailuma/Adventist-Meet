# 566 - Father We Thank Thee
-------------------
## Key Eb Major


**1**    
Father, we thank You  
For the light that shines all the day;   
For the bright sky You have given  
Most like Your heaven;  
Father, we thank You.  

**2**  
Father, we thank You  
For the lamps that lighten the way;  
For human skill's exploration  
Of Your creation  
Father, we thank You. 

**3**  
Father, we thank You  
For the friends who brighten our play;  
For Your command to call others  
Sisters and brothers;  
Father, we thank You. 

**4**  
Father, we thank You  
For your love in Jesus today,  
Giving us hope for tomorrow  
Through joy and sorrow;  
Father, we thank You.
